Our service explained

1

Exclusivity Ads

Most marketing companies try to get as many leads as possible. But that can be a problem. They get lots of leads who aren’t serious. Then they have to deal with a bunch of tire kickers, that are just looking around. Instead, we focus on finding the exact kind of customer you want to work with. For example, homeowners aged 35 to 65. We know which strategies work best and we’ll use the best performing ones for you.

2

Personal Secretary

Leads suck. So we are not going to give any until they are on your schedule ready to buy. Our US based sales team will call each lead to qualify them based on your conditions. The ones who have the budget, are looking to start, and is the homeowner, we’ll set on your calendar.

3

Rinse & Repeat Sales Process

We know you can sell. But, we want to build you a sales machine that lets anyone to sell like you do. This way, you and your team can consistently close 40% - 50% of appointments you go to. We’ll teach you what we learned from hiring over 200 sales reps between our partners. The things to say, the process to use, everything you need. Then, we’ll help you find the salespeople to hire.

4

8 Figure Operations

When you’re getting more jobs than you can handle. We’ll give you the systems you need to be able to scale to $10M per year, without having to work anymore hours than you are currently doing. We’ll teach you how to find team members, how to attract them, and make them stay. We’ll even teach you how to optimise your profits, open more offices, and everything you need to scale.
